OUR STORY

Our congregation comes with the experience and knowledge

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God. He walked this earth healing, teaching, performing miracles and setting an example for us all to follow on our journey back to Heaven. Jesus Christ said, “He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do.”

This statement is as true today as it was 2,000 years ago. We teach principles and techniques of Christian meditation, healing and prayer.

there are many aspects to healing which may be at least as important as the medicinal aspect. Prayer, meditation and Bible study are highly effective. The understanding of the mind/body link and the significance of positive and negative thoughts and emotions upon our health is crucial.

We believe in the existence of the Spiritual Gifts as outlined by Paul in 1 Corinthians 12, especially the gift of healing.

This organization exists to provide an avenue and support for people who are sincerely seeking the deeper Christian Knowledge and personal relationship with our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. Our purpose is to establish a fellowship of Christians, Interdenominational; for studying the Bible to understand the mysteries and develop the Spiritual Gifts enumerated by Paul: healing, prophecy, miracles, wisdom, etc

We use the gifts for healing, comfort and uplift all who seek our aid.
